# Maintenance Management

Tools for scheduling preventive maintenance, tracking repairs, and managing vehicle lifecycles.

## Overview

Maintenance management software helps fleet operators keep vehicles in optimal condition, reduce downtime, and extend vehicle lifespan through systematic tracking and scheduling.

## Key Features

### Preventive Maintenance Scheduling

- Mileage-based reminders
- Time-based scheduling
- Engine hour tracking
- Custom maintenance intervals

### Repair Tracking

- Work order management
- Parts inventory
- Labor cost tracking
- Vendor management

### Compliance & Inspections

- Safety inspection scheduling
- Regulatory compliance tracking
- Documentation storage
- Audit trails

## Open Source Solutions

### Fleet Maintenance Pro

Open-source fleet maintenance tracking system.

- **Features**: Work orders, PM scheduling, parts inventory
- **License**: Open source

### CMMS Solutions

Computerized Maintenance Management Systems (CMMS) adaptable for fleet:

- **Maintenance Assistant**: Open-source CMMS
- **OpenMAINT**: CMMS for facility and asset management

## Maintenance Best Practices

1. **Regular Inspections**
   - Pre-trip inspections
   - Post-trip inspections
   - Periodic safety checks

2. **Scheduled Maintenance**
   - Oil changes
   - Tire rotations
   - Brake inspections
   - Fluid checks

3. **Documentation**
   - Maintain service history
   - Track warranty information
   - Record parts used

4. **Cost Tracking**
   - Monitor maintenance costs per vehicle
   - Analyze trends
   - Budget forecasting

## Integration with Fleet Management

Maintenance management systems often integrate with:
- GPS/telemetrics systems (mileage, engine hours)
- Fuel management systems
- Accounting software
- Parts suppliers
