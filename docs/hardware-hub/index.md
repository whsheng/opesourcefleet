# Hardware Hub

Welcome to the **Hardware Hub** — a comprehensive catalog of open hardware and compatible devices for fleet management systems.

## Overview

The Hardware Hub provides information about GPS trackers, OBD-II devices, gateways, sensors, and other hardware compatible with open-source fleet management platforms.

## Categories

### GPS Tracking Devices

Dedicated GPS tracking hardware that reports location, speed, and other vehicle data.

### OBD-II Devices

Plug-and-play devices that connect to the vehicle's OBD-II port for diagnostics and tracking.

### Gateways & Routers

Communication devices that enable connectivity between vehicles and fleet management platforms.

### Sensors & Accessories

Additional hardware like fuel sensors, temperature sensors, dashcams, and driver identification systems.

## Open Hardware Initiatives

### OpenTracker

Open-source GPS tracking hardware designs.

- **License**: Open Hardware licenses
- **Features**: Customizable firmware, community-driven development

### CAN Bus Open Projects

Open-source projects for CAN bus communication and vehicle diagnostics.

## Hardware Selection Guide

When selecting hardware for your fleet:

1. **Compatibility**: Ensure device protocols are supported by your software
2. **Connectivity**: Choose between cellular (2G/3G/4G/5G), satellite, or LoRa
3. **Installation**: Consider plug-and-play vs. hardwired solutions
4. **Power**: Battery-powered, vehicle power, or hardwired
5. **Durability**: IP rating for environmental protection
6. **Certifications**: FCC, CE, PTCRB certifications for regulatory compliance
